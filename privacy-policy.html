Privacy Policy

At CASE, your privacy is of utmost importance to us. We understand that the information you share with us is personal and sensitive, and we are committed to safeguarding your data. Our Privacy Policy provides a comprehensive overview of how we collect, use, and protect your information. We believe in transparency and want you to feel confident about how your data is handled.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services. This information may include:

a. Personal Information

When you create an account or interact with our platform, we may ask you to provide certain personal information, such as:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Username and password
  • Profile information (e.g., bio, profile picture)
  • Payment information (if applicable)

b. Usage Data

We may collect information about how you access and use CASE. This data helps us understand user behavior and improve our services. Usage data may include:

  • Your IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages you visit on our platform
  • Time and date of your visit
  • Time spent on those pages
  • Other diagnostic data

c. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to monitor activity on our platform and store certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data that may include an anonymous unique identifier. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To Provide and Maintain Our Service: We use your information to create and manage your account, provide customer support, and ensure the functionality of our platform.
  • To Improve Our Services: We analyze usage data to understand how our platform is used and to identify areas for improvement. This helps us enhance user experience and develop new features.
  • To Communicate with You: We may use your email address to send you updates, newsletters, marketing materials, and other information that may be of interest to you. You can opt out of receiving these communications at any time.
  • To Ensure Security: We use your information to monitor for potential security threats and to protect the integrity of our platform.
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: We may use your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal requests.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

a. With Service Providers

We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our service ("Service Providers"), provide the service on our behalf, perform service-related services, or assist us in analyzing how our service is used. These third parties have access to your personal information only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

b. For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

c. In Business Transfers

If CASE is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your personal information may be transferred. We will provide notice before your personal information is transferred and becomes subject to a different Privacy Policy.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement reasonable measures to protect it from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These measures include encryption, firewalls, and secure server environments.
